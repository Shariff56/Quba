About Our Madarasa

Welcome to Al-Huda Madarasa, a premier Islamic educational institution dedicated to providing quality Quranic and Islamic studies to children and youth in our community. Established in 2005, we have been at the forefront of Islamic education, combining traditional teaching methods with modern educational techniques.

Our mission is to develop well-rounded Muslims who are grounded in their faith, strong in character, and prepared to contribute positively to society. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes Quran memorization, Arabic language, Islamic studies, and character development.

Our qualified teachers are passionate about Islamic education and committed to nurturing each student's spiritual and intellectual growth in a caring, supportive environment.

Our Programs

Quran Memorization (Hifz)

Our comprehensive Hifz program helps students memorize the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed. We offer both full-time and part-time options with individualized attention.

Arabic Language

Learn to read, write and speak Arabic with our structured program. We teach Modern Standard Arabic as well as Quranic Arabic to understand the Holy Quran.

Weekend Islamic School

Our weekend program covers Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, Hadith and Islamic manners. Perfect for school-going children to complement their regular education.
